5th December 2014

Stylish thinking & growing at DBC...

So, I am a visual thinker...and also a reading thinker. Essentially I learn best through alot of reading & great visuals - which means that in the world of high tech I still pull out a pencil and paper!

I was unsurprised but encouraged by the findings of my Learning Style quiz - I always drew things when trying to explain my thinking & now I had a reason to do it! During Phase 0 I have really tried to embrace this urge to use paper even when it feels 'wrong' given how much onscreen work we are doing. I have found that taking notes as I read & most importantly after I finish coding assignments has helped me to remember what I have learnt and put it in context. I have also gone super old school and printed out cheatsheets for sublime, git & the command line - something about seeing and touching it in front of me helps me remember.
